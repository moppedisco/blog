About

I'm born in Algeria, raised in Sweden but now living in Zurich, Switzerland for the past 4 years working for Education First as the guy bridging the gap between design and development.

Before joining the world wide web professionally I was working as a school photographer and had my own photostudio. I've studied Information and Communication Technology and Computer Science at the Royal Institue of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden.

When im not working you'll find me surfing, playing football or flipping burgers for my friends.
