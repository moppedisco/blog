A Stockholm restaurant guide

Not being one of the lucky ones working at a cool company that offers free meals, eating at a restaurant for lunch once or twice a week is pretty standard issue. The next step in the process usually evolves around the questions where and what to eat.

Unfortunately most "restaurant guide sites" dont focus on small neighborhood restaurants, fast food joints or cafe that serve food so your search will most likely be an incomplete one.

On those premises i decided to try building my own restaurant guide.
