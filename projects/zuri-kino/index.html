Better cinema sites

www.zuri-kino.ch

Navigating websites in a foreign language obviously has its difficulties. With me currently living in Switzerland this has become present in my daily online life when checking restaurant opening hours, buying movie tickets etc.

The latter one, finding what movies run at the cinemas, if found especially annoying. The crook here was cineman.ch, Switzerlands biggest movie site. Tons of ads and poor information structure I found myself opening new tabs for each movie to compare movie data and simultaneously googling their IMDB rating. Web 2014 can surely be done better.

Objectives

  • Show IMDB data.
  • Showcase movies in a sexier way.
  • Be able to compare movies in the same view.
